2012 | 2 Seasons | Mystery, Comedy

Gravity Falls is a witty and mysterious animated series that follows twins Dipper and Mabel Pines as they spend the summer with their great-uncle Stan in the quirky town of Gravity Falls. Together, they uncover secrets, solve mysteries, and face supernatural challenges, making the series a perfect blend of humor and intrigue.

Creator: Alex Hirsch

Starring: Jason Ritter, Kristen Schaal, Alex Hirsch, Linda Cardellini
