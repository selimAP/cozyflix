2017 | 6 Seasons | Comedy, Family

Young Sheldon is a prequel to "The Big Bang Theory" that chronicles the early life of Sheldon Cooper, a gifted child growing up in East Texas. The series explores Sheldon's struggles with his extraordinary intellect, navigating high school at the age of 9, and dealing with his eccentric family, including his religious mother, George Sr., Missy, and his siblings.

Creators: Chuck Lorre, Steven Molaro

Starring: Iain Armitage, Zoe Perry, Lance Barber, Montana Jordan, Raegan Revord, Annie Potts
