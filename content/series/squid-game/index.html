2021 | 2 Season | Drama, Thriller

"Squid Game" is a South Korean survival drama series that tells the story of hundreds of cash-strapped contestants who accept a strange invitation to compete in children’s games. Inside, a tempting prize awaits—with deadly high stakes.

Creator: Hwang Dong-hyuk

Starring: Lee Jung-jae, Park Hae-soo, Wi Ha-jun, Jung Ho-yeon
