Rick and Morty is an animated series that explores the bizarre adventures of an eccentric, alcoholic scientist, Rick Sanchez, and his good-hearted but easily influenced grandson, Morty Smith. Together, they traverse the multiverse, encountering alien worlds, strange beings, and alternate realities, blending dark humor, satire, and sci-fi tropes into their escapades.

Creator: Justin Roiland, Dan Harmon

Starring: Justin Roiland, Chris Parnell, Spencer Grammer, Sarah Chalke
