1999 | 22 Seasons | Comedy, Animation

Family Guy is an animated sitcom that follows the adventures of the Griffin family in the fictional town of Quahog, Rhode Island. Led by the bumbling yet well-meaning Peter Griffin, the family includes his wife Lois, their three kids—Meg, Chris, and baby Stewie—and their talking dog, Brian. The series blends outrageous humor, satirical takes on pop culture, and absurd situations across its long-running seasons.

Creator: Seth MacFarlane

Starring: Seth MacFarlane, Alex Borstein, Seth Green, Mila Kunis, Mike Henry
