2021 | 3 Seasons | Comedy, Animation

The Great North is an animated series that follows the adventures of the Tobin family in Alaska. Led by single dad Beef Tobin, the family navigates life, love, and laughter in the northern wilderness while dealing with quirky neighbors, hilarious situations, and heartwarming moments.

Creator: Wendy Molyneux, Lizzie Molyneux, Minty Lewis

Starring: Nick Offerman, Jenny Slate, Will Forte, Dulcé Sloan
