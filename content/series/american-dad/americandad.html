2005 | 20 Seasons | Comedy, Animation

American Dad is an animated sitcom that centers on the eccentric Smith family in the fictional town of Langley Falls, Virginia. Led by CIA agent Stan Smith, the family includes his liberal wife Francine, their two kids—Hayley and Steve—their sarcastic pet alien Roger, and a talking goldfish named Klaus. The show combines satirical humor, political commentary, and outlandish scenarios.

Creators: Seth MacFarlane, Mike Barker, Matt Weitzman

Starring: Seth MacFarlane, Wendy Schaal, Scott Grimes, Rachael MacFarlane, Dee Bradley Baker
