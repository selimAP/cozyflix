"The Big Bang Theory" is an American television sitcom that follows the lives of socially awkward physicists Leonard Hofstadter and Sheldon Cooper, and their friends Penny, Howard Wolowitz, and Raj Koothrappali, as they navigate work, friendship, and romance in Pasadena, California.

Creator: Chuck Lorre, Bill Prady
