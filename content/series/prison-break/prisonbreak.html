2005 | 5 Seasons | Action, Drama, Thriller

Prison Break follows Michael Scofield (Wentworth Miller), who devises an elaborate plan to break his brother, Lincoln Burrows (Dominic Purcell), out of prison after he is falsely accused of murder. Facing intricate conspiracies and dangerous challenges, the series unfolds across five intense seasons, blending action, suspense, and emotional drama.

Creator: Paul Scheuring

Starring: Wentworth Miller, Dominic Purcell, Sarah Wayne Callies, Amaury Nolasco, Robert Knepper
