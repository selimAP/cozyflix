2011 | 2h 7min | Action, Drama, Sci-Fi

"Real Steel" is a heartwarming and action-packed story set in a future where robot boxing is a popular sport. Charlie Kenton, a washed-up fighter, teams up with his estranged son Max to train an underdog robot named Atom. Together, they fight for redemption and family amidst high-octane battles.

Director: Shawn Levy

Starring: Hugh Jackman, Dakota Goyo, Evangeline Lilly, Anthony Mackie, Kevin Durand
