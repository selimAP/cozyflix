2001 | 2h 37m | Biography, Drama, Sport

*Ali* explores the life of boxing legend Muhammad Ali, focusing on his triumphs and challenges both in and out of the ring. The film delves into his transformation, struggles with racial injustice, and his commitment to his beliefs during pivotal moments in his career.

Director: Michael Mann

Writers: Stephen J. Rivele, Christopher Wilkinson, Eric Roth

Starring: Will Smith, Jamie Foxx, Jon Voight, Mario Van Peebles
