1992 | 2h | Comedy, Family

In this sequel, Kevin McCallister (Macaulay Culkin) finds himself stranded in New York City after accidentally boarding the wrong flight during a family vacation. With his dad's credit card in hand, Kevin enjoys the luxury of the Plaza Hotel, but his adventure takes a turn when he encounters the Wet Bandits, Harry (Joe Pesci) and Marv (Daniel Stern), who have escaped prison. Kevin must once again outwit the bumbling criminals using his clever traps while discovering the importance of kindness and family.

Director: Chris Columbus

Writer: John Hughes

Starring: Macaulay Culkin, Joe Pesci, Daniel Stern, Tim Curry, Catherine O'Hara, John Heard
