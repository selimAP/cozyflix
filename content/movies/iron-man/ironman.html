2008 | 2h 6m | Action, Sci-Fi

**Iron Man** tells the origin story of Tony Stark, a billionaire industrialist and genius inventor who is kidnapped and forced to build a devastating weapon. Instead, he builds a high-tech suit of armor to escape and vows to use his technology to protect the world as the armored superhero, Iron Man.

Director: Jon Favreau

Writers: Mark Fergus, Hawk Ostby, Art Marcum, Matt Holloway

Starring: Robert Downey Jr., Gwyneth Paltrow, Terrence Howard, Jeff Bridges
