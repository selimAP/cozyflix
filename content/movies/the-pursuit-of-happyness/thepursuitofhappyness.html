2006 | 1h 57m | Biography, Drama

The Pursuit of Happyness is a heartwarming drama based on the true story of Chris Gardner, a struggling salesman who faces financial hardship and homelessness while raising his young son. Despite numerous challenges, Chris’s determination and resilience lead him on a remarkable journey toward a better life.

Director: Gabriele Muccino

Producers: Todd Black, Jason Blumenthal, Steve Tisch, James Lassiter

Starring: Will Smith, Jaden Smith, Thandiwe Newton, Brian Howe
