2001 | 1h 46m | Action, Crime, Thriller

The Fast and the Furious is a high-octane action film that introduces us to the underground world of street racing. LAPD officer Brian O'Conner goes undercover to investigate a series of truck hijackings and infiltrates the street racing crew led by Dominic Toretto. As Brian gets drawn into the exhilarating lifestyle and forms bonds with the crew, he faces tough choices between his duty and his newfound loyalty.

Director: Rob Cohen

Starring: Paul Walker, Vin Diesel, Michelle Rodriguez, Jordana Brewster
