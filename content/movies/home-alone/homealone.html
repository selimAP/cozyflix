1990 | 1h 43m | Comedy, Family

Eight-year-old Kevin McCallister (Macaulay Culkin) is accidentally left home alone when his family goes on a Christmas vacation. Initially thrilled to have the house to himself, Kevin soon discovers that two bumbling burglars, Harry (Joe Pesci) and Marv (Daniel Stern), are planning to rob his home. Using his wit and an array of booby traps, Kevin must defend his house and outsmart the burglars while rediscovering the true spirit of family and Christmas.

Director: Chris Columbus

Writer: John Hughes

Starring: Macaulay Culkin, Joe Pesci, Daniel Stern, Catherine O'Hara, John Heard
