Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone introduces us to Harry Potter, an orphaned boy who discovers on his eleventh birthday that he is a wizard. He embarks on a magical journey to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he befriends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger. Together, they unravel the mystery of the Philosopher's Stone and face the dark wizard Voldemort.

Director: Chris Columbus

Writer: J.K. Rowling (novel), Steve Kloves (screenplay)

Starring: Daniel Radcliffe, Emma Watson, Rupert Grint, Richard Harris
