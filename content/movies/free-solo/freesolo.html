2018 | 1h 40m | Documentary, Adventure, Sport

Free Solo is a breathtaking documentary that follows professional climber Alex Honnold as he attempts to conquer the first free solo climb of the famed El Capitan's 900-metre vertical rock face at Yosemite National Park. With no ropes or safety equipment, this visually stunning and deeply emotional journey reveals the physical and mental challenges of one of the most daring feats in human history.

Directors: Elizabeth Chai Vasarhelyi, Jimmy Chin

Producer: Evan Hayes

Starring: Alex Honnold
