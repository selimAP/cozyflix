1997 | 3h 14min | Drama, Romance

Titanic is a timeless romantic drama that tells the tragic story of the ill-fated RMS Titanic. The film centers around Jack, a free-spirited artist, and Rose, a young woman from an upper-class family, who meet and fall in love aboard the ship. Their romance blossoms against the backdrop of social barriers and the impending disaster that would change their lives forever.

Director: James Cameron

Starring: Leonardo DiCaprio, Kate Winslet, Billy Zane, Kathy Bates, Frances Fisher
