2021 | 1h 42min | Animation, Family, Musical

Encanto tells the story of the Madrigal family, who live in a magical house in the mountains of Colombia. Each family member has a unique magical gift, except for Mirabel. When the family's magic is in danger, Mirabel might be the only hope to save their enchanted home.

Directors: Byron Howard, Jared Bush

Starring: Stephanie Beatriz, María Cecilia Botero, John Leguizamo, Mauro Castillo
